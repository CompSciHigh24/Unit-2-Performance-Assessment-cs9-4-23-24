About Us

Who Are We?

Project Anime is an anime-based store. We sell merch based off any anime that we have available here, as obvious as it sounds.


Our Purpose?

We’re planning on creating an anime website that ships overseas because we think that there should be more opportunities for people all over the world to receive merchandise that only ships to a specific country. We’re solving the issue of region-locked merch so that everyone in other countries can get this merch as well.
